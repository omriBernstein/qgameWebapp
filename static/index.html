About qromp

Quantum computer programming is challenging, and its explanations are often riddled with general abstractions about parallel universes or such. That's all well and good, but hopefully this website can be a tool for learning about it "hands on". The aim of this quantum computer simulator is to provide both an IDE and learning environment for quantum programming. Our primary goal, realized or not, is to bring quantum computer programming to a wider audience in a more digestible, immersive, and learnable way.

This project began as a Hampshire College Division III project and we hope to move forward from there. Inspiration and ideas are taken heavily from Bret Victor's fantastic article: Learnable Programming.

To contact me with feedback or hatemail (or both), feel free to email ob08@hapmshire.edu.
